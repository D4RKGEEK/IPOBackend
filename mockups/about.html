Methodology

We read the filing so you don't have to.

IPORadar turns 300-page SEBI prospectuses into structured, traceable data. Every number you see links back to a section of an actual DRHP, RHP or final prospectus — with a confidence score attached.

How the data flows

1

Discover filings

We monitor SEBI, BSE and NSE for new DRHP / RHP / prospectus filings across MainBoard and SME segments.

2

Parse & section

Each document is split into named sections (capital structure, objects, financials, risk factors) and converted to clean markdown.

3

Extract structured fields

Promoters, financials, allocations and ratios are extracted into a unified record, each field tagged with its source section and a confidence score.

4

Publish & version

Records publish only after review. Every refresh bumps a version so you always know how fresh the data is.
